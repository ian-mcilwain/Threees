Roll the Dice!

About

Threes is a dice game for two or more people. Players roll 5 dice three times to try to attain the lowest score. Threes count as zero when counting the score. After the first roll the player must keep the value or "stay" on at least one of the five dice. After the second roll the player must stay on the value of at least two of the dice (including the one kept from the first round). After the third and final roll, the score is counted.

Threes is traditionally a gambling game played with US dollar bills. Substitute Loonies, Pound coins, Euros or whatever equivalent works where you are.

If you aren't into casual low-stakes gambling with your friends, use it to figure other stuff out like who is going to call for takeout... I dunno.

How to Play

Throw money in the pot

If it is the first round, click the "One Die" button to roll a single die to figure out who will roll first. Else, Winner of the last round rolls first.

Click or tap on a dice to stay on that value

After the first roll, the player must "stay" on at least one die

After the second roll, the player must "stay" at least two dice, including the die from the first roll.

After the third roll, a players turn is over, and the score is tabulated. Threes count as zero, and all others count as their face value.

A player joins by throwing a dollar (or whatever) in the pot

Start by rolling a single dice, whoever gets the lowest roll starts the game
